Our team of instructors

Marko

Meet Marko, your seasoned spearfishing instructor with a passion for the ocean that runs as deep as the depths he dives. Born and raised in a coastal town nestled between rugged cliffs and azure waters, Marko's affinity for the sea was ingrained from a young age. With sun-kissed skin weathered by countless hours spent beneath the waves, Marko exudes a quiet confidence honed through years of mastering the art of spearfishing. His keen eye for marine life and understanding of underwater ecosystems make him not only a skilled hunter but also a knowledgeable steward of the ocean. Marko's journey into spearfishing began as a childhood fascination, but it quickly evolved into a way of life. He has since traversed the world's oceans, from coral reefs teeming with colorful fish to remote islands where the ocean stretches endlessly into the horizon. As an instructor, Marko is patient and thorough, guiding novice divers through the intricacies of breath-hold diving and the art of stalking prey beneath the surface. His emphasis on safety is unwavering, ensuring that every student learns not only how to catch fish but also how to respect and protect the marine environment they inhabit. Under Marko's tutelage, students learn the delicate balance between hunter and conservationist, understanding that spearfishing is not merely about catching fish but about forging a deep connection with the ocean and its inhabitants. Whether you're a beginner seeking to dip your toes into the world of spearfishing or an experienced diver looking to refine your skills, Marko is the guide you can trust to lead you on an unforgettable journey beneath the waves.
